Establishment

The Fizz was established in 2012 by four guys interested in sports, burgers and good drinks.
We wanted to have a friendly establishment downtown that served these principles.
Our goal is to help bring diversity and fun to the Albuquerque area.

Owners

We the owners of the Fizz always wanted to own a bar and grill near UNM.
We want our bar and grill to be as friendly as possible.
We love albuquerque and the International Ballon Fiesta and encourage patronage.
